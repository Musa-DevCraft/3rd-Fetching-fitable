Weekly Training Schedule

Day Exercise Details
Monday 💪 Arms and Chest → Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio (jumping jacks, high knees)
→ Push-Ups: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
→ Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
→ Arm Circles: 3 sets of 10 reps (clockwise and counterclockwise)
→ Diamond Push-Ups: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Tuesday 🍑 Glutes and Legs → Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio (jumping jacks, high knees)
→ Squats: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
→ Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
→ Glute Bridges: 3 sets of 12 reps
→ Donkey Kicks: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
Wednesday 😴 Rest Day Rest
Thursday 💪 Arms and Chest (Variation) → Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio (jumping jacks, high knees)
→ Push-Ups: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
→ Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
→ Arm Circles: 3 sets of 10 reps (clockwise and counterclockwise)
→ Diamond Push-Ups: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Friday 🍑 Glutes and Legs (Variation) → Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio (jumping jacks, high knees)
→ Squats: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
→ Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
→ Glute Bridges: 3 sets of 12 reps
→ Donkey Kicks: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
Saturday 💪🏋️‍♂️ Full Body Workout → Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio (jumping jacks, high knees)
→ Bodyweight Circuit: Perform each exercise for 45 seconds with 15 seconds rest in between.
→ Repeat the circuit 3 times (Jumping Jacks, Mountain Climbers, Bodyweight Squats, Push-Ups, Plank)
→ Cooldown: 5 minutes of stretching focusing on all muscle groups
Sunday 🚶‍♂️🌄 Active Rest Day → Engage in light activities such as walking, cycling, or yoga to keep your body moving without intense exercise

Diet Plan

Ensure you're consuming enough protein to support muscle growth.
Include sources like:

  1. 🍖 Lean meats
  2. 🍳 Eggs
  3. 🥛 Dairy
  4. 🍒 Legumes
  5. 🥜 Nuts


Also, incorporate plenty of:

  1. 🍎 Fruits
  2. 🥕 Vegetables
  3. 🌾 Whole Grains

Stay hydrated throughout the day by drinking 💦 water 🌊 regularly.
